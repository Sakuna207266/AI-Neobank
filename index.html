Shortfall Detection & Action Plans

When a gap is detected, Vero doesn't just tell you — it calculates the specific earnings boost you'd need and identifies when and where to get it, while there's still time to act.

  • Gap calculated to the dollar, not a vague warning

  • Alerts 3–7 days before the due date
  • Based on your personal earnings history

  • City-level demand signals as user base grows

Product Preview

Credit designed for cash-flow reality

Dynamic Credit Limits

Limits set and updated based on your rolling income and spending data — not a static annual income figure that goes stale the moment you change platforms.

Volatility-Aware Repayments

Minimum payments that can flex with your earnings in a given period. In a slow week, your minimum scales accordingly — then recovers in better weeks.

Conservative Start, Data-Driven Growth

We start with conservative limits and expand them as your track record deepens. Less risk for you. Less risk for us. Better outcomes for everyone.

No T4 Required

Gig income is verified directly through platform connections. Your months of earnings history is the proof — no tax documents needed at application.
